About

Enjoy modern authentic Japanese dining at Kuroinu in Liverpool, London and Manchester. The a la carte menus provide a superb selection of Japanese dishes, prepared by our outstanding head chef Maria Takeuchi and her talented team.

Each venue has its own unique decor designed by the renowned Toshiki Kadomatsu creating a modern Japanese and contemporary dining experience at Kuroinu. Whether it's a meal with friends or a business lunch, for the ultimate Japanese dining experience join us at Kuroinu.
